Support

Help for StillForm.

StillForm is a private body-progress journal for iOS and Android. It may appear as Silhouette Frame in some early app metadata while the public brand transition finishes.

Contact

Email support at eetttble@gmail.com.

For App Store or Play Store review, this is the best support contact for account, privacy, purchase, and technical questions. StillForm does not require an account or login.

When You Email

  • Include the app version from Settings.
  • Include your device model and iOS or Android version.
  • Describe what you expected and what happened.
  • Do not send progress photos unless support explicitly asks and you choose to share them.
