Privacy Policy

Private by default, useful by choice.

This policy applies to StillForm, also listed in some builds as Silhouette Frame. StillForm is a single-user body-progress journal built around local storage, weekly photos, measurements, notes, comparisons, and optional AI reads.

Last updated: May 8, 2026

Short Version

StillForm does not require an account. Your progress photos, weight logs, measurements, notes, goals, and saved reads are stored on your device by default. The app has no ads, no social feed, and no advertising trackers.

Data leaves your device only when you take an explicit action or turn on an explicit setting: asking AI for a read, enabling crash reports, enabling detection diagnostics, using purchases, exporting or sharing data, importing selected photos, or contacting support.

Data Stored On Your Device

The following data stays in the app's private storage on your iPhone or Android device by default:

  • Progress photos, calibration photos, goal photos, and imported photos.
  • Weight logs, measurements, notes, goals, reminders, and app preferences.
  • AI reads saved in the local journal after you ask for them.
  • Pose and body-analysis data generated on-device to help with alignment, comparisons, and body-scan views.

Derived body masks and cutouts are generated on-device where supported, cached locally, and treated as derived photo data. They are used for app visuals and, when available, may be used to reduce the surrounding environment shown in an AI photo request that you explicitly start.

Uninstalling the app removes its local data from the device. StillForm does not provide cloud backup or account sync in the current app.

Optional AI Reads

AI reads are opt-in per action. StillForm does not upload photos or journal data just because the app is open.

Compare AI

When you ask AI to evaluate a photo comparison, StillForm may send the compared photos, pose labels, dates, comparison context, recent app history, relevant notes, prior saved reads, and an anonymous per-install identifier for rate limiting. Face cropping is enabled by default before photo upload. When a body cutout is available, the request may use the cutout instead of the original photo to reduce room and background detail.

Insights AI

When you ask AI to evaluate Insights, StillForm sends a bounded snapshot of logged numbers and dates, such as recent weigh-ins, measurements, shoot dates, pose counts, focus phase, goals, notes, and prior saved reads. This path does not send photos.

AI Processing Providers

AI requests are sent over HTTPS through a Cloudflare Worker operated for StillForm and forwarded to Anthropic for model processing. AI inputs and outputs are not used by Anthropic to train models under Anthropic's API data-use terms. Standard provider logs may be retained for security, abuse prevention, debugging, or billing according to each provider's policies.

If you do not want any data sent for AI processing, do not tap the AI read buttons. The app remains usable without AI.

Crash Reports

Crash reports are off by default. If you turn them on in Settings, StillForm may send anonymized crash diagnostics to GlitchTip so crashes can be fixed. Crash reports can include stack traces, app version, device model, and operating system version. They are scrubbed to avoid photo paths, body data, names, email addresses, request headers, and user-entered content.

You can turn crash reports off at any time in Settings.

Detection Diagnostics

Detection diagnostics are off by default. If you turn on "Help improve detection" in Settings, StillForm may share numeric capture diagnostics used to improve body-presence and pose reliability. These diagnostics can include detector scores, pose labels, camera-facing mode, quality scores, gate results, platform, app version, and a random per-install identifier.

Detection diagnostics do not include progress photos, face pixels, raw body keypoints, photo file paths, or raw shoot identifiers. You can turn this off and request deletion of shared detection rows from Settings.

Purchases And Subscriptions

StillForm may use Apple or Google in-app purchase systems and RevenueCat to show plans, process purchases, restore purchases, manage subscription status, and unlock paid features when available. Purchase systems and RevenueCat may receive store transaction metadata, product identifiers, entitlement status, platform, app version, and an anonymous purchase identifier.

StillForm does not send RevenueCat your photos, notes, measurements, AI reads, email, name, advertising ID, or custom subscriber attributes.

Photo Library, Export, And Sharing

Importing past photos uses the system photo picker. StillForm reads only the photos you select and copies them into the app's private storage.

Exporting, saving, or sharing data is user initiated. When you export or share, the selected data leaves the app through the system share sheet or the destination you choose.

Support Emails

If you email support, the contents of your email and your email address are received by the support mailbox so the request can be answered. Do not send progress photos unless support asks and you choose to share them.

Children's Privacy

StillForm is not directed to children under 13, or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Your Choices

  • Do not tap AI read buttons if you do not want AI processing.
  • Turn crash reports off in Settings.
  • Turn detection diagnostics off in Settings.
  • Use the detection-data deletion control in Settings when available.
  • Delete individual items, recently deleted items, or all app data from Settings.
  • Uninstall the app to remove its local sandbox data from the device.
  • Manage or restore purchases through the app, the App Store, Google Play, or RevenueCat Customer Center when available.

Security

Local app data is protected by the operating system's app sandbox and device security model. Data sent to service providers is sent over HTTPS.

Third-Party Services

  • Cloudflare Workers for AI request proxying and opt-in detection diagnostics.
  • Anthropic for optional AI processing.
  • GlitchTip for opt-in crash reports.
  • RevenueCat, Apple, and Google for purchases and subscription status when purchase features are available.

StillForm does not use advertising networks, social-login providers, or tracking pixels.

Changes

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date will be revised. Material changes will also be reflected in future app releases when appropriate.
